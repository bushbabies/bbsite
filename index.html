The BushBabies

BushBabies are a collection of 2222 randomly generated nocturnal primates stored on the Solana blockchain. They were one of the most advanced and powerful civilizations-embracing the use of cryptocurrencies, memes, and building on the new internet- until one night, Mysterious Creatures showed up and forced them from their land. The Mysterious Creatures took control of everything the BushBabies owned, including the Gigatree, which was a tree as tall as the Empire State Building, acting as their headquarters. Now, the BushBabies must rebuild and gain the strength to take back their land, and the Gigatree, from the Mysterious Creatures that drove them away.

The Specs

The BushBabies are unique and computer generated from over 100 possible traits spread across 7 layers. The metadata is stored permanently using Arweave, a decentralized data storage. The price to mint a BushBaby will be 0.2 SOL and there will be no bonding curves, so everyone will have a fair chance to mint one at the same price. Holding a BushBaby acts as your membership to the community and grants exclusive access to the BushDAO. In the BushDAO we will focus on DeFi and NFT investments that the community agrees on, and profits from such investments shall be distributed across BushBaby holders. In the future, holders will be able to breed a GEN2 BushBaby for every GEN1 BushBaby they hold in their wallet, and will also be able to mint a Mysterious Creature after the story progresses and the Mysterious Creatures are revealed.
